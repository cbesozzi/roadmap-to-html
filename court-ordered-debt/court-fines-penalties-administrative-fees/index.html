Court fines, penalties & administrative fees

GETTING RID OF CIVIL ASSESSMENT CHARGES

If you don’t make payments on time or don’t go to court when you’re supposed to, the judge adds an extra fee of $300 to your debt. This is called a “civil assessment.” If the reason you didn’t pay or appear in court was because you were incarcerated, and you were charged with a civil assessment as a result, you should be able to get it waived by filing a “Petition to Vacate Civil Assessment.” This form is available from the court clerk. A sample form is also available in Appendix H, PG. 876.
