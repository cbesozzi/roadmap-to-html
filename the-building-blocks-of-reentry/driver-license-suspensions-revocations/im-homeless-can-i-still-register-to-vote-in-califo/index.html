I’m homeless. Can I still register to vote in California?

Yes. You don’t need a home address to register. Using the map on the bottom of the voter registration form, just identify 2 cross-streets where you usually stay. If you are registering online, you can check the box that says, “I do not have a street address” in the home and mailing address section.[258]
