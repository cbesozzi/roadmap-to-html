Notice to the District Attorney

    Before sending your application to the Governor, you must notify the District Attorney in the county where your conviction is from that you intend to request a pardon.[3103] There is a special form to use for this, which is included in the Application for Gubernatorial Pardon.
    You must deliver your notice for to the DA(s) at least 10 days before you submit your application to the Governor.[3104]
