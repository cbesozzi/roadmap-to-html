How do I transfer counties on mandatory supervision?

The rules and process for transferring your Mandatory Supervision to another county are the same as the rules that apply to informal and formal probation.[689] For more information on this process, see PG. 213.

How do I transfer states on Mandatory Supervision?

How do I transfer states on Mandatory Supervision?

The rules and process for transferring your probation, PRCS, or Mandatory Supervision to another state are the same as the rules that apply to state parole.[690] For more information on this process, see PG. 196.
