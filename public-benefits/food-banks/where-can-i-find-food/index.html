Where can I find food?

To find a food bank or other partner organization that gives out donated food near you, visit http://www.cafoodbanks.org/Hunger-in-CA or call the Association of California Food Banks at 1-866-321-4435.

You do NOT need to enroll in any public benefits programs to visit a food bank or partner organization for food.

But, you may wish to consider enrolling in the Emergency Food Assistance Program (see PG. 528 below), if you are eligible, as it is a government-assisted program for low-income people and their families.
