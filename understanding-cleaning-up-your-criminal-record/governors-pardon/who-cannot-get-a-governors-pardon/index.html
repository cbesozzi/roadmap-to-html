Who CANNOT get a governor’s pardon?

You are NOT eligible for a pardon if you ONLY have misdemeanor convictions (that are NOT sex offenses) on your California criminal record.

Note: If you have a felony conviction from another state or for a federal conviction, you still do NOT qualify for a California Governor’s pardon. Your felony or misdemeanor sex offense conviction must be from California to qualify.
