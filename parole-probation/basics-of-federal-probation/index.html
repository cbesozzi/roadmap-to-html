Basics of Federal Probation

What is federal probation?

After you are convicted of a federal crime, federal probation is often used as an alternative sentence to prison time.[755] That means that federal “probation” is considered a sentence in itself. For the most part, if you are placed on federal probation, you must report to your assigned probation office and comply with all the terms and conditions (rules) of your federal probation.[756]
