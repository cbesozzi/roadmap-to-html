Arson Registration

Arson Registration

Will the remedy remove my registration requirement?

    Expungement—YES, an expungement will remove your arson registration requirement if:
    Your conviction was for a misdemeanor arson offense AND you get the conviction dismissed under California’s expungement statute.[3172]
    Certificate of Rehabilitation—YES, this will remove your arson registration requirement, even if you had a lifetime registration requirement![3173]
    Pardon—YES, this will remove your arson registration requirement.[3174]
    BUT remember, a pardon is very difficult to get and granted only in “extraordinary circumstances.”
