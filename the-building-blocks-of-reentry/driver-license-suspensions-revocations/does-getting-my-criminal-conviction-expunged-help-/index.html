Does getting my criminal conviction expunged help me get my suspended or revoked driver license back?

No. Unfortunately, an “expungement” (a California dismissal) won’t get your California driver license back if the DMV suspended or revoked it.[201] The only way to get your driver license back is to satisfy the requirements of the DMV.
