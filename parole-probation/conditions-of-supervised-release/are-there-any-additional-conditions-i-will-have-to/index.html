Are there any additional conditions I will have to follow on supervised release?

Most likely, yes. The U.S. Sentencing Commission recommends that the “Standard Conditions” be applied to everyone on Supervised Release (this is the same as for Federal Probation conditions, discussed earlier on PG. 241),[832] See the question “WHAT DISCRETIONARY CONDITIONS WILL I HAVE TO FOLLOW ON SUPERVISED RELEASE?” on PG. 254 for more information.
