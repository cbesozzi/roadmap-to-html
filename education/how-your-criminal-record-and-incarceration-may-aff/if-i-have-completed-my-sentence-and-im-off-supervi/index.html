If I have completed my sentence, and I’m off supervision, what barriers might I still face?

Once you have completed your sentence, including any term of supervision, most of the barriers that we have discussed so far will no longer exist for you. Unfortunately, your criminal record may still inhibit or interfere with your educational plans. See PG. 1045
