What are the rules for violations & revocations of formal probation?

See PG. 228 for the rules and procedures. Probation revocation proceedings are the same for informal probation (PG. 210), formal probation (PG. 215), and a newer form of supervision called “Mandatory Supervision” (PG. 224).
