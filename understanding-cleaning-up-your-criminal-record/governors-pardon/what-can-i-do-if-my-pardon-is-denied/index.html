What can I do if my pardon is denied?

If your pardon is denied, the only thing you can do is start over and file a new application. However, just as with a Certificate of Rehabilitation, you should not file a new application until AFTER you fix or improve whatever problems caused your first application to be denied.[3115]

Sealing California State Adult Arrest Records

What happens to my RAP sheet if my arrest record is sealed?

If your arrest record is sealed, all information about the arrest is deleted from your RAP sheet, and a new RAP sheet is issued without any information about the arrest. ALL records from the arrest are destroyed, including your fingerprint card.When your arrest record is sealed, the arrest (and anything associated with it) is considered NEVER to have happened.
