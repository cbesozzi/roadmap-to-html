Why would a U.S. Passport useful? Why might I need one?

If you have a U.S. passport, you can lawfully travel outside the United States and return home by air, sea, or land. A passport can be useful if you need to visit family abroad, especially in case of an emergency like illness or death.[203] A passport also counts as a government-issued photo ID for all purposes. For these reasons, if you are eligible OR when you become eligible for a U.S. passport, it is a good idea to get one!
