What happens if the BPH decides to continue my parole?

If the BPH decides to continue your parole past your PDD, you should be reviewed for possible discharge each year until your maximum discharge date (MDD) is reached.[399] At these annual review dates, you will remain on parole unless the BPH takes action to discharge you.[400]
