What court will hear my case?

The court in the county in which you are supervised has “jurisdiction” (the legal authority) to hear the motion or petition to modify, revoke, or terminate you probation.[708]

For those on probation, it will be either the court in the county in which you are supervised or the court in the county in which the alleged violation of supervision occurred.[709]
