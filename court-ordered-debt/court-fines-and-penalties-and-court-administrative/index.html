Court fines and penalties and court administrative fees

Court fines and penalties and court administrative fees

As discussed on PG. 819, court fines and penalties are debts you may get as punishment or consequences for the crime you were charged with. Court administrative fees are paid for the daily operation of the court. While these two debts are different, here these debts are discussed together because the court usually adds these fees together and requires you to pay them together and to one office.
