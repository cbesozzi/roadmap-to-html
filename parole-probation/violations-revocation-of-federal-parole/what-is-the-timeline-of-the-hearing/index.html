What is the timeline of the hearing?

A local revocation hearing must be within 60 days of the probable cause determination. Institutional revocation must be held within 90 days of the date the warrant was executed, when you were taken into custody. However, you can request postponement or consent to a postponed revocation proceeding.[980]
