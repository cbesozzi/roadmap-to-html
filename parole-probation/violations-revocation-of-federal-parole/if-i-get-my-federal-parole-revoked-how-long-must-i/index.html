If I get my federal parole revoked, how long must I serve before the parole commission reviews my case again?

The U.S. Parole Commission uses its own guidelines to determine the length of time you should serve in prison for the revocation. The guidelines are the same ones used for inmates who apply for their initial parole hearings. Decisions, of course, can be made above or below the guidelines for good cause (a good reason).[988]
