Will I definitely get into government-assisted housing if I am successful in challenging the initial denial?

No, not necessarily. If you are successful, you could get admitted into housing, but you might just get another review of the facts or another hearing—which could help you get in, or could still result in denial.[1240] What you will get, if successful, is a fairer hearing.
