How long is my supervision under federal probation?

The maximum term of probation that can be imposed depends on the type of conviction offense:[761]

FELONY CONVICTION: Minimum 1 year to maximum 5 years; or

MISDEMEANOR CONVICTION: Maximum 5 years.

INFRACTION: Maximum 1 year.[762]

Therefore, to know the range of time you could be sentenced to federal probation, you will need to know if you were convicted of a felony, misdemeanor, or infraction. If you aren’t sure, ask your federal public defender or your probation officer.
